About

Chakravyuh is the most awaited event of Thapar Institute Of Engineering & Technology, and rightly so, as it is the first mega event from CCS. Hosted every odd semester and running for an entire week, the event gives the budding technocrats of TIET a chance to prove their mettle at the vanguard of technical competitions and further improve upon their skills.
